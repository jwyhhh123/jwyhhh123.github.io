What is Artificial Intelligence

WY J
2019-06-02 (Updated: 2019-08-24)
Year1/ Artificial Intelligence

人工智能已经不是新鲜课题，它是计算机算计学科的一个分支。传统意义上人们对这门学科有四种理解:

  • 像人一样思考 think humanly
  • 像人一样行动 act humanly
  • 合理地思考 think rationally
  • 合理地行动 act rationally

这四种理解意味着人们将从四种方向去研究和发展智能。而研究的结果，将会是一种具有创造性，自我学习和语言能力的机器。但是，当前大学教材主要采用“合理地行动”这种理解，旨在设计能够获取周围环境信息,作出合理的行为以获得最优解的计算机程序。

Artificial Intelligence is on its way. As a branch of Computer Science, it is defined:

part 1 Think humanly
part 2 Act humanly
part 3 Think rationally
part 4 Act rationally

The diversity of its definitions reveal that people will study in it from different perspectives. And the result of research is a machine with creativity, self-learning and language ability. In university, however, researchers are more willing to adopt “act rationally” for a robot which optimises its behavior in a certain environment.
