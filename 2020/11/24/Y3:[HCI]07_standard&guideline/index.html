Design standards and guidelines

WY J
2020-11-24 (Updated: 2020-11-24)
Year3/ Human Computer Interaction

Standards

More focus on hardware than software

ISO 9241 defines usability as: effectiveness, efficiency and satisfaction with which
specified users can achieve specified goals in a particular environment.

Accessibility:
Def: designing pruduct to be used by many different people

Web Accessibility Principles - WCAG 2.0

  • Perceivable
  • Operable
  • Understandable
  • Robust

Principles vs Guidelines

Principles:

  1. Rules of thumb
  2. Vague, high level

Guidelines

  1. Concrete recommendations

Principles are about what to aim for, for example aesthetic integrity.
Guidelines are about how to, for example font style and margin of pixels.

Design patterns

• patterns allow designers to reuse what works
• provide a language for designers to collaborate
• communicate design problems & solutions
• captures essence of how to solve a type of problem
