What is design

Design challenge: Design is not to find the answer but to find possible answers.

  • A tradeoff between often conflicting requirements
  • Aesthetic as well as functional
  • Design is time-consuming
  • Design is hard to get right
  • Easy to overlook

Def: process of creative and critical thinking that allows information and ideas to be organized, decisions to be made, situations to be improved, and knowledge to be gained. It is about to fit the user, task, and context of use.

Design is:

  1. A discipline that eplores the dialogue between products, people and contexts.
  2. A process that defines a solution to help people achieve their goals
  3. An artifact produced as the result of solution definition.
  4. the iterative decision-making process employed in the creation of a product or system.
  5. It is an object with a sensory or emotional appeal beyond its usefulness

Design as:

  • Styling
  • Idea (hidden from you)

Everywhere: something you less noticed but indead is an outcome of conscious thought processes and decisions.

Brainstorming
Stage 1:
1] Melee(融合) of ideas and concepts
2] Collect all ideas (expansion phase)
3] Rules:

  • No criticism
  • Any idea
  • Be Brief

Stage 2: Understand, Refine, Cluster

  1. Comment on ideas, remove less useful ones
  2. Organise: cluster by topic, approach etc
  3. Discuss, elaborate
  4. Record decisions, routes to follow up
  5. Keep originals

Lateral thinking
Def: what could be rather than what is possible

Scamper technique:

  1. Substitute: what can I change for my product
  2. Combine: creativity sometimes is about combine some unrelated things
  3. Adapt: can I adapt from somewhere or someone
  4. Modify: _make larger, color, style…-
  5. Put to Another Use: can we let someone else use, make it use in other context
  6. Eliminate: simplify, e.g. time…minor functions…
  7. Reverse” in different orders,closure…
